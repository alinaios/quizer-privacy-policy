Privacy Policy

AsanaQuiz

This Privacy Policy explains how Namaste collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

Effective date: May 3, 2026 Platform: iPhone, iPad, Mac

Overview

AsanaQuiz is a yoga learning app designed to help users recognize asanas, remember pose names, and track practice progress over time.

We aim to collect only the information needed to provide core app features such as sign-in, progress sync, reminders, premium access, and quiz content delivery.

Information We May Collect

1. Account information

If you sign in, we may receive information associated with your account, such as your email address, authentication identifier, and basic profile details provided by the sign-in service.

2. Practice and progress data

The app stores quiz attempts, completed practices, streaks, review history, study preferences, and related learning progress.

3. Purchase and entitlement information

If you unlock premium features, the app may store purchase entitlement status so your access can be restored and recognized on your device or account.

4. Notification preferences

If you allow reminders, the app stores your reminder preferences and may use local notification permissions to send practice reminders.

5. Technical and diagnostic information

Third-party service providers such as Apple, Firebase, or Google may process limited technical information needed for authentication, syncing, configuration, content delivery, or app functionality.

How We Use Information

  • To provide quiz content and learning features.
  • To save and sync your progress across sessions or devices.
  • To support sign-in and account-related functionality.
  • To restore premium access and purchased features.
  • To send practice reminders if you enable notifications.
  • To improve app stability, reliability, and user experience.

Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted third-party services, including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Remote Config
  • Google Sign-In
  • Apple Sign-In
  • StoreKit for in-app purchases

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies and platform requirements.

Data Storage

Some data may be stored locally on your device. If you sign in or use sync-enabled features, some data may also be stored securely in cloud services used by the app.

Your Choices

  • You can choose whether to sign in.
  • You can choose whether to enable notifications.
  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can request account-related changes in accordance with platform or legal requirements.

Children's Privacy

AsanaQuiz is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate permission where required by law.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page with the updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the app publisher through the support information provided in the App Store listing or repository profile.
