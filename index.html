Privacy Policy

AsanaQuiz

This Privacy Policy explains how AsanaQuiz collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

Effective date: May 6, 2026 Platform: iPhone, iPad, Mac

Overview

AsanaQuiz is a yoga learning app designed to help users recognize asanas, remember pose names, and follow study progress over time.

We aim to collect only the information needed to provide core app features such as optional sign-in for cross-device progress sync, reminders, and Firebase-backed quiz content delivery.

Information We Collect

1. Account information

If you choose to sign in with Google, we may store only your email address and an authentication identifier associated with your account.

2. Practice and progress data

The app may store quiz attempts, practiced days, progress history, reminder settings, language selection, and design preferences.

3. Notification preferences

If you enable reminders, the app stores your reminder preferences and may use local notification permissions to schedule practice reminders.

4. Technical service data

Firebase, Google Sign-In, Apple, and other platform services may process limited technical information needed for authentication, syncing, configuration delivery, and app functionality.

How We Use Information

  • To provide quiz content and study features.
  • To save progress across devices only when you choose to sign in.
  • To support account and sign-in functionality.
  • To schedule practice reminders if you enable notifications.
  • To maintain app reliability and core service functionality.

Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted third-party services, including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Remote Config
  • Google Sign-In

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies and platform requirements.

Data Storage

Some data may be stored locally on your device. If you sign in or use sync-enabled features, some data may also be stored in cloud services used by the app.

Your Choices

  • You can use the app without signing in.
  • You can choose whether to enable notifications.
  • You can sign out at any time.
  • You can contact us about privacy-related questions or account-related requests.

Children's Privacy

AsanaQuiz is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate permission where required by law.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
